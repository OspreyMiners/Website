Mars Tier Sponsors

Stenner Pumps

A very generous donation from the Stenner Pump Company carried the Osprey Miners through most the 2016 mechanical design process.

UNF CCEC

The Osprey Miners could not exist without the support of UNF's College of Computing, Engineering, and Construction.

Renaissance Jax

Renaissance Jax has provided us with outreach opportunities, as well as manufacturing space and expertise. RJax leads the development of FIRST FLL, FTC, and FRC teams in Northeast Florida.

The Florida Space Grant Consortium provided the team with the final funding needed to complete the 2016 NASA RMC Robot!
